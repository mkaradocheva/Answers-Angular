Our Rules

In order everyone to spend nice time in our rooms, please, respect our rules.

Good codebase and in-built powerful standards

  1. Please be kind, supportive and respectful to every participant of the chat at all times.
  2. Please do not use language that is graphic, inappropriate, flirtatious or sexually suggestive. Additionally, refrain from sharing links of this nature.
  3. Please refrain from sharing personal contact details or social media.
  4. For moderation purposes, please only use English in this space.
  5. If you find that another participant is not following one of these rules, please contact us rather than engaging in conflict of any sort.
  6. Please do not share names of drugs or medication with others.
  7. Please ensure you do not have an username that is abusive or inappropriate.
  8. To ensure a positive environment in group support, all users must be sober to participate.
  9. Sending SPAM messages or attempting to clog the room by over-posting is not allowed.
